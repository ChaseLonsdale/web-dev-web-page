Common questions

Things people usually ask us.

How long does a typical project take?

Starter projects run 3–5 weeks. A full Studio engagement is typically 8–12 weeks. Brand + Web packages vary based on scope and run 12–18 weeks. We'll confirm your timeline before any work begins.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes. We enjoy working at the zero-to-one stage — it's where design decisions carry the most weight. We'll help you prioritise what matters most given your runway and goals.

Can we hire you for just design, not development?

Absolutely. Design-only engagements are common, especially when you have an in-house dev team. We deliver Figma files with full documentation, component specs, and a walkthrough session.

What's the payment structure?

We work on a 50/50 split for most projects — 50% to kick off, 50% on completion. Larger engagements use a milestone-based schedule. We never invoice for work before it's delivered.

Do you offer ongoing support or retainers?

Yes. Monthly retainers are available from $1,800/month and cover design updates, content changes, performance monitoring, and strategic advisory time.

How many revisions are included?

Each design phase includes two rounds of revisions. In practice, our process is collaborative enough that most projects come in well under that. We'd rather get it right than rush to a sign-off.
